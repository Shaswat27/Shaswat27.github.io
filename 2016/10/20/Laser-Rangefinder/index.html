Laser Rangefinder

My friend Siddharth Jha had thought of an idea to use a laser and a webcam to mimick a 2D LIDAR and had registered for IBM Day a while back.The date for the presentation was 24th September, two days after our mid-semester exams. He asked me for demostrating the project with him and to help with the conversion of laser range data to a point cloud format compatible with ROS (robot operating system). The project seemed interesting and easily doable within 2 days, so I agreed. But the time-frame wasn’t quite as planned. The thing to note here is that both of us are lazy creatures with nothing but impending deadlines getting us of our backs. Not surprisingly, nothing was done on the 22nd.
